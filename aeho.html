Aeho Solutions:
Product Information Management

Aeho is a PIM system designed for retailers and brands with large quantities of product information across multiple channels including e-commerce sites, catalogs, mobile devices, in-store, etc.

Being the only designer at the beginning of the project, I collaborated with Aeho team to define the scope and timeline for the MVP and created a backlog for design.

My Role

Research, user flows, wireframes, UX strategy, UI/UX design, visual design, prototyping, brand identity

GOALS

Centralization

Enable users to effortlessly manage their product information in one place (including assets like multimedia, images, documents and more)

Shareability

Allow users to securely share product information with partners and teammates

Easy to Use

Empower users to take control of their data from importing, exporting and sharing

The Design System

I defined the visual design (logo, typography, color and illustration) for the project early on and build the Design System within a Sketch component library to ensure that all design elements were consistent.
